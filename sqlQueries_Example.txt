
- This an example query. Before the first query can be any text
- An exception are three dashes because they are used to identify start of queries
- Text on the same line after the three dashes is put on the select sheet of Excel


--- Total Primary Energy Supply 

- Comment text is allow before the first SELECT or TRANSFORM (please use UPPER case letters) 
- No text is allowed after the query until the next three dashes or end of file

  TRANSFORM Sum(VALUE) 
  SELECT PRODUCT AS Technology
  FROM [WEO2024_AnnexA_Free_Dataset$] 
  WHERE SCENARIO = <SCENARIO>  
    AND PRODUCT NOT IN ('Total','Renewables','Coal','Natural gas', 'Oil: non-energy use')
    AND FLOW = 'Total energy supply'
    AND CATEGORY = 'Energy'
  GROUP BY PRODUCT
  PIVOT YEAR
  

--- Total final consumption 

  TRANSFORM Sum(VALUE) 
  SELECT PRODUCT AS Technology
  FROM [WEO2024_AnnexA_Free_Dataset$] 
  WHERE SCENARIO = <SCENARIO>  
    AND PRODUCT NOT LIKE ('Total%')
    AND FLOW = 'Total final consumption'
    AND CATEGORY = 'Energy'
  GROUP BY PRODUCT
  PIVOT YEAR


--- Total CO2 removed 

  TRANSFORM Sum(VALUE) 
  SELECT FLOW AS Technology
  FROM [WEO2024_AnnexA_Free_Dataset$] 
  WHERE SCENARIO = <SCENARIO>  
    AND FLOW IN ('Direct air capture: offsite', 'Biofuels production')
    AND CATEGORY IN ('CO2 DAC stored','CO2 process stored')
  GROUP BY FLOW
  PIVOT YEAR
